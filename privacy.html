Privacy Policy

Timesheet Assistant respects your privacy. This extension only requests the minimal necessary Google permissions:

  • ./auth/calendar.readonly – to view events and holidays.
  • ./auth/calendar.events – to create or update logged tasks as calendar events.

All timesheet data is stored locally in your browser extension unless explicitly exported by you. We do not share personal data with third parties. This app complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy.

If you have questions, please contact us at support@timesheetassistant.com.
